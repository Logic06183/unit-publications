Research Categories

The bibliography is organized into 10 thematic categories:

  1. Agriculture (13 publications)
  2. Animal Studies (96 publications)
  3. General Climate Change (31 publications)
  4. Health Systems (6 publications)
  5. Human Physiology (14 publications)
  6. Impacts on Human Health (not MNCH) (30 publications)
  7. Infections and Infectious Diseases (30 publications)
  8. Maternal, Foetal, Neonatal, Infant and Child Health (39 publications)
  9. Policy and Practice (50 publications)
  10. Social, Environmental Determinants of Health (19 publications)

Each section contains numbered references with complete citation information including authors, titles, journals, publication years, and page numbers. The research spans over five decades (1969-2025) and covers diverse topics related to climate change impacts on health, from physiological responses in humans and animals to policy interventions and social determinants.

For more information about this research or to request specific publications, please contact the Wits Climate and Health Research team directly.
